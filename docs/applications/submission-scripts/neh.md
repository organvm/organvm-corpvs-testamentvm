**Deadline:** March 6, 2026 (confirm exact date on NEH site — deadlines vary by program)
**Award:** Stipend (typically $1,200–$3,300 depending on program duration)
**Fit:** 6/10 — credential-building, not high dollar
**Identity position:** Educator (11 years, 100+ courses, 2,000+ students)
**Benefits cliff:** SNAP-safe (stipend is small). Below all thresholds on $12K base.

---

> NEH Summer Programs for College and University Educators fund seminar-style institutes and workshops for humanities faculty. The application typically requires a short essay connecting your teaching and research to the program's theme. These are credential-building: "NEH Summer Program Participant" on a CV signals humanities credibility. The stipend is secondary.

### Selecting the Right Program

Look for programs with themes touching:
- **Digital humanities** — your 97-repo system IS digital humanities infrastructure
- **Pedagogy and creative practice** — 11 years teaching writing + creative technology
- **Interdisciplinary approaches** — the eight-organ model spans theory, art, commerce, community
- **AI and the humanities** — the AI-conductor methodology is a humanities contribution

If no program theme is a strong fit, skip this cycle. NEH runs Summer Programs annually.

## If Something Goes Wrong

- **Not sure if adjunct qualifies:** NEH defines eligible applicants as "college and university teachers." Adjunct instructors at accredited institutions qualify. If the form asks for "rank" or "title," use "Adjunct Instructor" or "Lecturer."
- **No program theme fits:** Skip this cycle. NEH runs Summer Programs annually. Don't force a bad fit — your time is better spent on higher-fit applications (Creative Capital, WFF Housing, Headlands).
- **Application asks for institutional letterhead:** Ask your most recent employer for a brief letter confirming your teaching role. If that's not possible, provide your teaching history with dates and institutions.
- **Word limits tighter than expected:** Cut the third paragraph first (classroom application). The first two paragraphs carry the essential credential + research narrative.
- **They ask about published research:** The 42 essays and 404K-word corpus IS published research. Frame it as: "42 published nonfiction essays constituting a sustained research program into creative systems design, AI-augmented production, and governance as artistic medium."
