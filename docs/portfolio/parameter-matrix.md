### 2. Premium content ↔ funnel inversion
**Observation:** Hokage has premium content (3 tiers) but no funnel. Elevate Align has funnel but incomplete premium content (GHL quiz stalled).

### 3. Domain-pair cross-pollination
**Observation:** Portfolio Operator fires three cross-flows per pair: (a) meta-skill extraction (writing/voice transfers Scott→Jessica), (b) domain-pair overlaps (Hokage pedagogy ↔ Jessica education), (c) tool/audience cross-flow (BODI's GHL stack ↔ Hokage's membership).

**Source:** `project_artifact_cross_pollination_master_diagnosis.md`
