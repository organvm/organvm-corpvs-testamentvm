## The "Body > Organs > Flat" Model

The current `registry-v2` is non-descriptive and generic. We must move toward a more fundamental ontological layout.

### Flattening Strategies
- **Body:** The total system root.
- **Organs:** Functional groupings (Theoria, Poiesis, etc.).
- **Flat Modules:** Below the organ level, modules should be as flat as possible.
- **Defense of Existence:** Every directory must submit a defense of existence and relativity to avoid bloat. If a directory cannot justify its independence, it is pulled elsewhere toward a felt unity.

### Constraints & Standards
- **Naming:** Every item requires a name that is unique and not generic. (Avoid `repo-registry.json` if possible, find the "True Name").
- **Semver:** Standardize versioning across all modules.
- **Max Depth:** Research the minimum required nesting for technical function (e.g., node_modules, .git).
